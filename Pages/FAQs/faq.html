Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with cooking
If you're a beginner, we recommend starting with simple, beginner-friendly recipes. Look for recipes that don’t require too many ingredients or complicated techniques. You can also check out our basic difficulty filter on the recipes page to easily find dishes that are perfect for novice cooks.

How do I prevent food from sticking to the pan when cooking?
To prevent food from sticking, preheat your pan before adding oil or butter. Once the oil is hot (but not smoking), add your ingredients. Non-stick pans help, but with stainless steel, make sure the pan is hot. Don't overcrowd the pan, as it can cause food to steam and stick.
What’s the best way to store fresh herbs to keep them from wilting?
To keep herbs fresh, trim the ends and place them in a jar with some water, like a bouquet. Cover loosely with a plastic bag and store in the fridge. You can also wrap them in a damp paper towel and store in an airtight container. For basil, keep it in water at room temperature since it doesn’t like the cold.

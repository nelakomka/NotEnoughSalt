Mamalyga with Mujdei

Romania | Lunch and Dinner

20 min
4 People
Save

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the salt and slowly stir in the cornmeal. Reduce heat to low and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens (about 5-10 minutes).
  2. Once thickened, remove from heat and set aside to cool slightly.
  3. For the Mujdei (garlic sauce), heat the vegetable oil in a small pan. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the vinegar. Pour the garlic sauce over the mamalyga and mix well.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and serve hot!

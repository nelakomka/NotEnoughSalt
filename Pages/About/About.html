Our Mission

At Not Enough Salt, we believe that cooking should be fun, accessible, and rewarding for everyone. Founded in 2024, we set out to create a space where home cooks of all skill levels can find recipes that inspire them, as well as helpful tips and techniques that make cooking easier and more enjoyable.


We are a team of passionate food lovers, experienced chefs, and home cooks who are dedicated to making cooking simple, approachable, and fun. Our mission is to provide you with everything you need to cook confidently, whether you're preparing a quick weeknight dinner, planning a weekend feast, or experimenting with new flavors.

Our Philosophy

At the heart of our platform is the belief that cooking should be simple, enjoyable, and accessible to all, whether you're a beginner or a seasoned enthusiast. We aim to streamline the cooking process by providing everything you need in one place—eliminating the frustration of endless searches and giving you instant access to the information you require, exactly when you need it.


We are committed to building a welcoming, global community where food lovers can connect, learn, and grow together. Our goal is to inspire individuals to not only enhance their cooking skills but also expand their palates and deepen their appreciation for the rich diversity of cuisines around the world.


Through this website, we strive to bring people closer together—fostering a love for cooking and travel that transcends borders and creates meaningful experiences in the kitchen and beyond. Our mission is simple: to help you discover the joy of cooking and the beauty of shared meals, all while ensuring that our platform is accessible and easy to navigate for everyone.


Why Choose Us?

  • For All Skill Levels: Whether you're just starting out or you're a seasoned cook, our recipes and tips are designed to meet you where you are. We offer recipes with different levels of difficulty, and our easy-to-follow instructions ensure success every time.
  • Diverse Recipe Collection: From comfort food to international cuisine, vegan to keto, we have a wide range of recipes to suit any taste, dietary restriction, or occasion.
  • Community-Oriented: We're more than just a website - we're a community of food enthusiasts! Join our newsletter for updates, share your recipes with us, and connect with fellow cooks on our social media channels.
